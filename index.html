About Tipig

Tipig is a Tagalog word meaning 'to save' or 'to preserve.' This collection celebrates the beauty found in imperfect memories—the blurred photographs, the overexposed moments, the slightly out-of-focus scenes that somehow capture more truth than technical perfection ever could.

Each photograph in this collection represents a memory preserved not for its technical excellence, but for its emotional resonance. Like the artifacts in a museum, these images are curated moments that speak to the universal human experience of remembering, of holding onto fleeting time, of finding profound beauty in the imperfect and ephemeral.

In our age of digital perfection and filtered reality, Tipig stands as a testament to the power of authentic moments. These photographs invite contemplation, asking viewers to slow down and appreciate the subtle poetry found in everyday imperfection.
