About Tipig

Tipig is a personal photography project dedicated to preserving memories in their raw, unedited form. The word "tipig" speaks to careful collection and preservation—a gathering of moments as they truly were.

Unlike traditional photography that often seeks perfection, this collection embraces imperfection. Each photograph is displayed exactly as it was captured—no cropping, no color adjustment, no editing. In this way, the images remain authentic to the memories they represent.

As you move through this exhibition, I invite you to view each image not just as a photograph but as a preserved moment in time—a memory made tangible, complete with all its beautiful imperfections.
